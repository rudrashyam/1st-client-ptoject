Regional Account Manager is responsible for handling product sales of an assigned region. A well-drafted Regional Account Manager Resume mentions the following duties and tasks – maintaining relationship with stores and distributors of the region, seeking new opportunities and making additional sales, acting as primary relay node between the distributors and the company, updating company’s product knowledge, developing presentations to market and sell new products, acquiring sales data and analyzing trends of sales; and gathering information about the competitor’s sales and market share. Candidates for this post should possess a degree in marketing and have an equivalent amount of work experience. They should be highly knowledgeable about the company’s products and depict proficiency in communicating well with clients.
